Oops you're on fire, your fireworks are going off now. That's it. That's the whole mod.

### When does this happen?

- Burning in fire? **Bang!**
- Sitting in a campfire? **Boom!**
- Struck by lightning? **Kaboom!**
- Swimming in lava? **Pew pew!**
- Magma blocks? **Pop!**
- Ghast fireball? **Bang!**
- Creeper explosion? **Boom!**

### Is this useful?

No.

### Is this funny?

Yes.

### How to use

1. Put fireworks in your pocket.
2. Step in lava.
3. ?????
4. **PROFIT.**
